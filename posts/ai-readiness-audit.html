What Is an AI Readiness Audit?

← Back to Blog

Published: June 2025

An AI Readiness Audit is the first step in understanding how AI can be applied effectively and securely in your organization. It identifies key workflows, bottlenecks, data opportunities, and low-risk automation areas—often uncovering quick wins you can act on immediately.

For charities and SMEs, this means faster reporting, smarter donor engagement, reduced admin time, and data-backed decisions without needing in-house developers or AI staff.

It typically includes:

  • Workflow analysis & pain points
  • Tool suitability assessment
  • Security & compliance checks
  • ROI and quick-win estimates
